The fearless Pepe is on his way through the desert, but he needs your help.
It is said, that there live crazy chickens and a very angry, big chicken also called as "el pollo loco".
Help Pepe to collect as many bottles and coins as possible and try to avoid the chickens. You can destroy them by jumping on them.
When you come across "el pollo loco" defeat it by shooting it with tabasco sauce.
Good luck!
